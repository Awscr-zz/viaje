San Francisco

San Francisco is often called "Everybody’s Favorite City," a title earned by its impressive scenery, cultural attractions, diversity, and world-class cuisine. San Francisco is filled with some of the best landmarks in the world like the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz.

ATTRACTIONS

Pier 39

A popular tourist attraction consisting of restaurants, rides, street performances, shops, and much more! Pier 39 should be at the top of your list of places to visit when in San Francisco.

Alcatraz Island

An offshore island that was originally a federal prison to some of the most dangerous prisoners in the United States. Take a ferry ride to the island day or night and explore the island at your own pace.

WHERE TO EAT

Super Duper Burgers

Looking for the best burgers in the city? The name brings on exactly what it says, taste anything from their "Ground Daily Natural Vegetarian" burgers to their "Chipotle Aioli" chicken sandwiches.

Pearl 6101

The best Mediterranean-inspired food that you can find in San Francisco, Pearl 16101 offers anything from seafood like their specialty cocktails to pasta like their "Ricotta Ravioli".

WHERE TO SHOP

Westfield San Francisco Centre

9 levels of over 170 shops and restaurants, there isn't anything that you can't find at the Westfield San Francisco Centre.

Union Square

A plaza located in downtown San Francisco, Union Square offers anything shopping, dining, and travel related.

Santa Monica

Santa Monica is an acclaimed beach city that is close to many other Los Angeles attractions. The Santa Monica Beach alone is the reason why many choose to visit Santa Monica, offering some of the best sunsets on the West Coast. It is really easy to get around the city, allowing you to experience the beautiful city to the fullest!

ATTRACTIONS

Santa Monica Pier

Looking to spend some time with the family at an amusement park? Explore an aquarium? Or even do some fishing? The Santa Monica Pier should be at the top of anyone's list of things to do when in Santa Monica.

Santa Monica Beach

Enjoy a beautiful time with the family at the Santa Monica beach. Why only swim when you can enjoy many other activities like seaside chess or taking a ride on the bike trail!

WHERE TO EAT

Birdie G's

Want a taste of some delicious cocktails or soup? Birdie G's has just what you're looking for, serving the best in one of the most beautifully designed restaurants in Santa Monica.

Umami Burger

Simply looking for the best burgers in town? Umami Burger has a strong reputation of serving the best food not only in Santa Monica, but also in other cities like New York City and Chicago.

WHERE TO SHOP

Main Street

Looking to spend your time browsing through the many never-ending local independent shops of Santa Monica? Not only can you shop, but you can also grab a bite, do some arts & crafts, and work on that summer body you've always wanted.

Santa Monica Place

Went swimming at the beach and got the last outfit of your trip wet? Walk a few minutes to the Santa Monica Place, an open-air shopping center with many well known stores and restaurants.

Los Angeles

Los Angeles is known as the entertainment capital of the world, with an endless amount of things to do. You've definitely seen the beauty of L.A in many T.V shows and movies, but there is nothing like taking a journey in one of the biggest cities of California.

ATTRACTIONS

Griffith Observatory

Known as "Southern California's gateway to the cosmos", people can view live shows in the Planetarium, enjoy the beautiful view of Los Angeles, or look through the night with a telescope.

Hollywood Walk of Fame

Are you a big fan of the Backstreet Boys? Mariah Carey? Johnny Depp? Talk a walk at the Hollywood Walk of Fame and browse the many stars that sit beneath your feet of your favorite musicians, actors, and much more!

WHERE TO EAT

Papilles

Get a taste of France at one of the best French restaurants in Los Angeles. Offering a huge selection of foods like pastas, salads, oysters, and much more!

Pizzana

In the mood for some pizza? Pizzana isn't just like any other regular pizza place in Los Angeles, they offer a great selection of specialty pizzas, pastas, and wine!

WHERE TO SHOP

The Grove

Often reffered to as the best shopping and dining experience in all of Los Angeles, The Grove offers places to shop at like Apple, Barnes and Nobles, and other well known retailers!

The Santee Alley

Looking for a unique outdoor shopping experience? Head to the Santee Alley where you can find over 150 retailers offering apparel, toys, perfumes, and much more!

Yosemite

Yosemite National Park lies in the heart of California. With its 'hanging' valleys, many waterfalls, cirque lakes, polished domes, moraines and U-shaped valleys, it provides an excellent overview of all kinds of granite relief fashioned by glaciation

ATTRACTIONS

Half Dome

Looking to take on the ultimate hike? Or just want to get some steps in? Half Dome is known as the prominent point of Yosemite, nearly 5,000 feet above the Yosemite Valley and 8,800 feet above sea level.

Yosemite Falls

Yosemite Falls is the highest waterfall in all of Yosemite, with a total height of 2,424 feet! Experience the breathtaking scenery at one of Yosemite's most visited spots!

WHERE TO EAT

The Ahwahnee Dining Room

The Ahwahnee Dining Room is an award-winning restaurant that is both magnificent and intimate. There are a great number of food selections to choose from developed by the hotel's executive chef based on traditional American cuisine.

The Mountain Room

The Mountain Room is not your average wine and dine, serving numerous dishes including sizzling steaks, fresh seafood, pastas, and much more. The restaurant not only serves some of the best food in Yosemite, but also has an amazing view of Yosemite Falls!

WHERE TO SHOP

Mountain Shop

Need to purchase some hiking gear? The Mountain Shop is the place to go if you forgot your hiking shoes, or your husband forgot to pack your favorite jacket.

Valley Lodge Gift Shop

Promised to bring back your sister a souvenir? The Valley Lodge Gift Shop is the place for all things gift related!

Sacramento

Sacramento is the capital of California and the 5th largest city in the state. It is located in north/central California, approximately an hour and a half northeast of Oakland and San Francisco. There are so many things to do in the city of trees!

ATTRACTIONS

Old Sacramento

A riverfront historic district with over 125 shopping, dining, events, and attractions, there is no other destination quite like the Old Sacramento Waterfront.

California State Capitol Museum

Home to the government of the U.S state of California, the California State Capitol building also serves as a museum. Experience California's rich history and witness the making of history through the modern lawmaking process.

WHERE TO EAT

The Boiling Crab

One of the greatest seafood spots in all of California, The Boiling Crab offers anything seafood like shrimp, king crab, clams, and even chicken nuggets.

South

It's pretty hard not to hear about one of Sacramento's greatest burger spots, South offers anything from chicken to sandwiches/burgers.

WHERE TO SHOP

Arden Fair

Arden Fair is the largest shopping mall in Sacramento, with over 165 stores including Disney, Apple, Macy's, and many more.

DOCO (Downtown Commons)

DOCO is where the tourists and locals like to hang out, a two story mixed-use enterntainment and shopping complex, with many retailers and restaurants to choose from.

San Diego

Located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean in Southern California, San Diego is widely known as “America’s Finest City.” Famous for its miles and miles of white-sand beaches and amazing weather, the city offers an abundance of fun attractions for visitors of all ages.

ATTRACTIONS

Balboa Park

Balboa park should be at the top of anyone's list of places to visit when in San Diego, a park that contains museums, theathers, the San Diego zoo, and much more.

USS Midway Museum

The USS Midway Museum is a historical naval aircraft carrier museum located in downtown San Diego. Experience and honor the legend of the USS Midway with many things to do!

WHERE TO EAT

Menya Ultra

In the mood for a taste of Ramen? Menya Ultra is an understated destination for Ramen lovers in San Diego, also offering many other Japenese foods.

Buona Forchetta

In search for the best pizza in town? Buona Forchetta is just the place! A tiny neighborhood italian restaurant that locals just can't get enough of!

WHERE TO SHOP

Seaport Village

Seaport Village is a waterfront shopping and dining complex right next to the San Diego Bay in downtown San Diego with over 70 shops, galleries, and restaurants.

Fashion Valley

One of the largest malls in all of California and the largest in San Diego, visit over 200 stores including designer houses like Gucci, Prada, Saint Laurent, and many more.

Lake Tahoe

Lake Tahoe, the clear, cobalt blue lake tucked into the snowy Sierra Nevada mountain range, welcomes athletes, adventurers and casual travelers to its easygoing and pine-scented atmosphere. A long sought-after vacation destination, Lake Tahoe keeps everyone’s interests piqued and their vacations active throughout the year.

ATTRACTIONS

Emerald Bay State Park

Looking to do some hiking, biking, or water sports? Emerald Bay is just the place to do all of the above!

Squaw Valley

Squaw Valley Resort is the 2nd largest ski area in Lake Tahoe and was the host for the 1960 Winter Olympics. Beginners and experienced skiers can all participate and have a good time!

WHERE TO EAT

Sprouts Cafe

Sprouts Cafe is a healthy restaurant that serves soups, salads, sandwiches, and much more. Sprouts Cafe aims to provide the best organic food in Lake Tahoe.

Lake Tahoe Pizza Company

The Lake Tahoe Pizza Company offers the best pizza that you can find in all of Lake Tahoe, they even have their very own signature pizza dough! Go checkout what everyone is talking about!

WHERE TO SHOP

Heavenly Village

Whether you are looking to enjoy the nightlife of Lake Tahoe, get some shopping done, or looking for a place to eat, Heavenly Village is the place to be when in Lake Tahoe!

San Jose

Nicknamed the capital of Silicon Valley, San Jose is a diverse area with an innovative spirit. While it has a distinct downtown, San Jose is a sprawling metro area that is as much defined by its suburban neighborhoods and large tech campuses as it is by the high-rises in its business district.

ATTRACTIONS

Winchester Mystery House

Once the home to Sarah Winchester, the widow of firearm magnate William Wirt Winchester, experience one of the most mysteriously haunted places in the United States!

The Tech Interactive

The Tech Interactive is a science and technology center located in downtown San Jose. How can you visit Silicon Valley and not discover the many technologies that this place has to offer!

WHERE TO EAT

The Boiling Crab

One of the greatest seafood spots in all of California, The Boiling Crab offers anything seafood like shrimp, king crab, clams, and even chicken nuggets.

Pizza Antica

Check out one of the best traditional pizza spots in San Jose, Pizza Antica offers foods served with the outmost top-quality ingredients.

WHERE TO SHOP

Santana Row

Santana Row is Silicon Valley's premiere destination for shopping, dining, living, and much more. Take a look at over 70 shops, 20 restaurants, and 9 spas and salons.

Westfield Valley Fair

With over 273 shops and restaurants, there isn't anything that you can't find at the Westfield Valley Fair shopping mall.

Anaheim

As a city built on imagination, Anaheim is where every unique and personal experience is sure to delight and inspire, how can you miss out on the perfect experience of taking a stroll through the wonderful city of Anaheim?

ATTRACTIONS

Disneyland

Disneyland or "the happiest place on earth", is no stranger to the world. With over 53 attractions, truly experience an amazing time with the family and never lose that smile.

Disney California Adventure Park

Just a 30 seconds walk from Disneyland, visit another amazing Disney theme park with over 37 attractions!

WHERE TO EAT

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. is no stranger to seafood lovers, serving not only the best seafood in Anaheim but also foods like burgers and soups.

The Pizza Press

Build your very own pizza in at Anaheim's most recognized pizza spot "The Pizza Press".

WHERE TO SHOP

Anaheim Plaza

Get some shopping done at the Anaheim Plaza, the perfect lifestyle destination.

Anaheim Town Square

Anaheim Town Square offers a great selection of shops, restaurants, and gyms including Gamestop, Chipotle, KFC, Ross, and much more.
